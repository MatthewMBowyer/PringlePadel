Ladies Beginner Social - R270

Every Sunday • 8AM–10AM @ Indoor Padel Revolution From 13 to 60 years old, our ladies’ beginner social brings women of all ages together for padel, laughter, and a community vibe like no other. Whether you’re a teen trying something new or rediscovering sport later in life, you’ll fit right in.

Expect the unexpected:

  • Fun themes like “Did someone say Mimosa?”, “Padeldon”, and “Hit it like it’s 1989”
  • Tips and tricks from Coach Lea to sharpen your game
  • A little bit of dancing between courts
  • Plenty of laughs, coffee, and new friendships

    • It’s not just padel — it’s a Sunday morning escape with the girls. Come as you are, leave with new skills, new friends, and a damn good time.

    Perfect for: ladies aged 13–60 wanting confidence, community, and lots of fun.
