This isn’t just coaching — it’s a full workout disguised as fun. In Padel X, every mistake becomes an opportunity

Double fault? 5 squats.

Volley behind the line? 5 lunges.

Missed smash? That’s 20 jumping jacks before you get back in the game.

By combining padel drills with functional fitness, you’ll learn faster, stay sharper, and build strength while you play. It’s high-energy, competitive, and guaranteed to leave you smiling (and sweating).
